Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

The short version: Jabe collects no personal data. Your conversations and journal entries stay on your device. We do not track you, sell your data, or require an account.

Overview

Jabe Wellness AI ("Jabe", "the app", "we") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the app handles, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Information We Do Not Collect

Jabe does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Your location
  • Your device identifiers
  • Your conversation history
  • Your journal entries
  • Any health or biometric data

What Stays On Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your iPhone using Apple's secure on-device storage and never leaves your device:

  • Chat session history
  • Journal entries
  • Mood tracking data
  • App preferences and settings

Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all of this data.

AI Conversations (Groq API)

Jabe uses the Groq API to power its AI responses. When you send a message, the text of your conversation is transmitted to Groq's servers solely to generate a response. This transmission:

  • Contains no personally identifiable information
  • Is not stored or used by Jabe beyond generating your response
  • Is subject to Groq's own privacy policy at groq.com/privacy

Jabe does not use your conversations for training, analytics, or any other purpose.

Microphone & Speech Recognition

If you use the voice input feature, Jabe requests access to your microphone and uses Apple's on-device speech recognition to transcribe your speech to text. Your voice audio is:

  • Processed locally on your device by Apple
  • Never recorded or stored by Jabe
  • Subject to Apple's privacy policy at apple.com/privacy

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, Jabe schedules a local notification entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to any external server.

Third-Party Services

Service Purpose Privacy Policy
Groq API AI chat responses groq.com/privacy
Apple SFSpeechRecognizer Voice-to-text transcription apple.com/privacy

Jabe does not use advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or any third-party tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Jabe is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last Updated" date.
